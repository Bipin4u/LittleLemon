About Us

Based in India, Little Lemon is a family-owned Northern restaurant focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Indian, Greek, and Turkish cultures and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Little Lemon is owned by Bipin Kumar, who moved to the United States to pursue his dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, Bipin relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in India.

Bipin handles all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Indian dishes to incorporate additional cuisines from the Northern region.
